# HelpAtlas:India Helplines Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

HelpAtlas:India Helplines is an offline reference app that helps users find public helpline numbers across India.

## 1) Information we collect

HelpAtlas does not require account registration and does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information.

## 2) Data stored on your device

If you use Favorites or Recent, those entries are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

## 3) Phone calls

When you tap a number, HelpAtlas opens your phone dialer using the selected number. HelpAtlas does not record, monitor, or store your call audio.

## 4) Helpline data accuracy

Numbers are compiled from official government sources and public directories. Numbers may change without notice. Always verify critical numbers with official authorities. In life-threatening emergencies, dial 112.

## 5) Third-party services

HelpAtlas does not include advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party personal data collection tools.

## 6) Children

HelpAtlas is intended for general audiences. Child helpline entries (for example, 1098) are provided for reference only.

## 7) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

## 8) Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer at infohelpatlas@gmail.com.
